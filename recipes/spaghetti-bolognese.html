Spaghetti Bolognese

Home

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti noodles and a rich, meaty tomato sauce. It’s hearty, flavorful, and easy to make for a quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

How to prepare

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook chopped onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti.
