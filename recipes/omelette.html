Omelette

Home

Description

A quick and simple egg dish made by beating eggs, cooking them in a pan, and folding in your favorite fillings like cheese, veggies, or herbs. Perfect for breakfast or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter or oil
  • Egg
  • Optional: cheese, tomatoes, onions, herbs

How to prepare

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper, and beat well.
  2. Heat a pan with butter or oil..
  3. Pour in the eggs and swirl to spread..
  4. Add optional fillings before the eggs fully set.
  5. Fold the omelette and cook for another minute. Serve hot.
