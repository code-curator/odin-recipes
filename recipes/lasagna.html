Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with fillings like meat or vegetables, tomato sauce, and a creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese. It's then topped with cheese and baked until golden and bubbly. Rich, hearty, and flavorful, lasagna is a popular comfort food around the world.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarela cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper

How to prepare

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles and set them aside.
  2. Cook ground beef, then add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with one egg and a little salt.
  4. In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, and cheese. Repeat the layers.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 minutes.
  6. Remove foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until the top is golden.
  7. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.
